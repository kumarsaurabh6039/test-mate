Leave Management

Hassle-free leaves for a happier workforce.

Say goodbye to email requests and confusing spreadsheets. Lovahr provides a transparent, easy-to-use interface for employees to request time off and for managers to approve it instantly.

  • Visual Balance Rings Employees immediately see their used, pending, and total balance for Casual, Sick, and Annual leaves.
